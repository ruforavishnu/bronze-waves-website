ROYAL LOGISTICS


Royal Logistics is one of Bronze Waves Trading Group, was founded with one purpose in mind – setting a new standard of communication and service in cargo transportation. This means we are keenly focused on providing rock-solid and reliable freight services and exceptional customer service, each and every time. Whether you have time-sensitive freight, or just need cost-effective transportation, Royal Logistics is the smart choice. From our white-glove handling to special rollouts to customer delivery requirements, we handle every logistical detail from beginning to end. We are proactive about communicating with our customers, keeping you up to date and informed about the progress of your shipment. No matter what your transportation need is, we’ve got you covered.

When Late Is Not an Option!!

Cargo transportation is complex with many steps and plenty of ways shipments can go awry. Royal Logistics manages all of the details so your freight gets to where it needs to be, when it needs to be there. On the rare occasion when an issue arises, our team will give you proactive, and resolve it as quickly as possible. Our end goal is ensuring you and your customers have a great experience.
